Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have the LocQar app?\u25bc
No worries! Receivers don't need the app. You'll get an SMS with a unique code.
How long can my package stay in the locker?\u25bc
Packages include 48-72 hours of free storage. After that, a small daily fee applies.
Is there a weight or size limit?\u25bc
No weight limits! If it fits in the locker, it ships. We have Small, Medium, and Large sizes.
How do I pay?\u25bc
We accept mobile money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), debit/credit cards, and in-app wallet.
